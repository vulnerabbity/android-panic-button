Notification panic button which can save you

Use of fingerprint or face authentication is not safe.
But typing password every time not much better especially in front of "security cameras".

This application displays notification with configurable panic buttons.

Features:
- Force lock panic action: allows to disable face and fingerprint authentication until enter password

Use cases:
- You in trouble
- You notice someone suspicious
- Police, goverment or bandits want you to unlock your smartphone

Future features:
- Sending http request to your server to notify your team, family, etc
